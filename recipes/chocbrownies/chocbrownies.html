Choc Brownies

Ingredients

  • Choc Brownie Mix
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Fillings (Nuts, Choc buttons etc.)

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 160C
  2. Melt Butter in the microwave
  3. Add dry mix and eggs to a bowl
  4. Add melted butter slowly and away from eggs so as to not cook them
  5. Mix thoroughly
  6. Add fillings as desired
  7. Add mix to a lined square baking tray
  8. Cook for approx 30 mins
  9. Enjoy!
