Chicken, Sausage, Peppers and Potatoes

A finished dish

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs, skin on and bone in
  • Spicy Sausage (Chorizo, Italian etc.)
  • Peppers (Bell peppers/Capsicum)
  • Potatoes
  • Other root vegetables (Carrot, Sweet Potato etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220C
  2. Lightly cook sausage until it can be cut cleanly
  3. Cut sausages and root vegetables into bite sized pieces
  4. Score Chicken thighs through skin down to the bone, 2-3 cuts per thigh should be plenty
  5. Place all items into a bowl/tray and add spices as desired
  6. Add oil and toss to coat evenly
  7. Spread evenly on a lined baking tray for ease of cleanup
  8. Roast at 220 for 60-70 minutes
  9. Allow to cool before distributing into containers for ease
