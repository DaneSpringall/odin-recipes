Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Cheese
  • Enchiladas
  • Tinned Tomatoes (Diced/Smashed)
  • Enchilada Sauce
    Steps
  1. Pre-heat oven to 180C
  2. Cook chicken breasts and then shred with a fork
  3. Mix chicken breasts with enchilada sauce
  4. Place shredded chicken mix on enchiladas, roll and place with seam at the bottom in a baking tray
  5. Top with remaining tomatoes and enchilada sauce, cover with cheese
  6. Place in oven for approx 30 minutes or until cheese is golden
  7. Remove from oven, allow to cool slightly and serve
